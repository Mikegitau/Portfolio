Habits

  1. Putting yourself in someone else’s shoes
    Why? Because we are not ‘right’ and other people ‘wrong.’ Genuinely trying to see the other person’s perspective can be liberating and mind opening.

  2. Embracing change
    3. Why? Because the future is inherently uncertain, and the ability to adapt to it by being open to new ways of doing things will keep you relevant and engaged.

  3. Embracing technology
    4. Why? Because it’s changing the world and, if we don’t move with it, we will become deskilled and irrelevant.

  4. Develop Concentration
    5. Why? Because we live in a world of distractions and concentration is one of the most valuable skills to develop to succeed in life.

  5. Positive Thinking
    6. Why? Because keeping your mind on building these positive habits, the more negative ones will start to drop away.

  6. Eating healthily
    7. Why? Because keeping hydrated is crucial for health and well-being.

  7. Cultivate a State of Abundance
    8. Why? Because scarcity mindset is the source of all suffering and I want to live a happy life.
